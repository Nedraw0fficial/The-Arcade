# Game Logo Assets

## Format
All game logos should be in **PNG format** with transparent backgrounds.

## Specifications
- **Size**: 400x400 pixels (recommended)
- **Format**: PNG with transparency
- **Style**: Bold, simple designs that work at small sizes
- **Colors**: Bright, neon colors that match the game's accent color

## How to Add New Logos

1. Create your logo as a 400x400px PNG with transparency
2. Save it to `assets/games/your-game-name/logo.png`
3. Update the game entry in `js/games.js` to point to your logo
4. The logo will automatically appear on the main hub!

## Design Tips

- Keep it simple - logos are displayed at various sizes
- Use high contrast against dark backgrounds
- Match the neon aesthetic of the arcade
- Test at different sizes to ensure readability
